This is a chronological list of projects I have worked on.

SplitEQ

SplitEQ is a groundbreaking EQ utilizing transient/tonal separation technology which offers a new approach to corrective and creative audio equalization.

Physion Mk II

Physion Mk II is a transient/tonal separation multi-effect engine, opening the door to practical mixing solutions and creative applications.

Arcade 2.0

Arcade 2.0 was released in 2021, which overhauled the audio engine of Arcade to include chromatic playback options to allow users to utilize Output’s vast high-quality sound library beyond the limitations of Arcade 1.0’s audio engine.

Thermal

Thermal is a popular saturation and distortion effects application launched by Output in 2020.

Portal

Portal is a popular granular effects application launched by Output in 2019.

Creating Audio Plugins with C++ and JUCE

Output Teaches Creating Audio Plugins with C++ and JUCE is a two-part online course which teaches students how to create audio applications using the JUCE C++ framework, which was released in 2019.

Arcade

Arcade, my second professional audio software project, initially released in 2018, has become Output’s flagship software product. This application is a living ecosystem of high quality sounds and presets delivered to users daily. Think Netflix, but for music.

Movement

Movement, my first foray into professional audio software development, was an instant success upon it’s launch in 2016.

MFA Works (2013 - 2015, Graduate Candidate)

My MFA Works from California Institute of the Arts are published online including a thesis titled, “The Algorithmic Beauty of Music: Approaches to Computer-Aided Algorithmic Composition.”
